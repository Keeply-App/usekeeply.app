Customer segments

Four states.
One clear picture.

Every customer is always in one of four states. The goal is keeping as many people as possible in Active — and catching At Risk before they become Lost.

New

Still early in their journey. No established rhythm yet — but this is the most critical window. The gap between first and second purchase determines whether someone becomes loyal or disappears.

Active

Buying within their normal rhythm. No action needed. Keeply watches quietly so you can focus on the customers who do need attention.

At Risk

Their rhythm has broken. They haven't left yet — but the window to bring them back is closing. This is the most actionable segment. The sooner you act, the higher the chance of recovery.

Lost

Silent for a long time beyond their personal rhythm. Recovery is unlikely but not impossible. Worth a long-shot attempt before writing them off.
