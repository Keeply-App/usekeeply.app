Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Keeply ("we", "us", "our") is a Shopify embedded app that predicts customer churn based on individual purchase rhythm. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a merchant.

1. Data We Collect

When you install Keeply, we access the following data from your Shopify store:

  • Customer data: first name, last name, email address, account creation date
  • Order data: order ID, processed date, total price, currency, financial status, cancellation status
  • Shop data: shop domain, currency code, timezone, installation date

We do not collect payment card information, passwords, or any data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data exclusively to provide the Keeply service:

  • Compute each customer's Average Days Between Orders (ADBO) — their individual purchase rhythm
  • Classify customers as Active, At Risk, Lost, or New based on their rhythm
  • Calculate Revenue at Risk for your dashboard
  • Display customer segments and details inside your Shopify Admin

We do not sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Fly.io (region: US East). Access is restricted to the Keeply application only. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as Keeply is installed on your store. When you uninstall Keeply:

  • Customer and order data is deleted immediately upon receiving the app/uninstalled webhook
  • Remaining shop metadata is deleted within 48 hours upon receiving the shop/redact webhook

5. Access Logging

Every read and write of customer or order data is logged with a timestamp, shop identifier, and operation type. Logs are retained for 30 days.

6. Your Rights

As a merchant, you can:

  • Request a data export: Contact us at the email below and we will provide a copy of all data we hold for your store
  • Request deletion: Uninstalling the app triggers automatic deletion. You may also contact us directly to request early deletion
  • Opt out: Uninstalling Keeply stops all data processing immediately

If a customer on your store requests a data report or deletion under GDPR or similar regulations, Shopify will forward the request to us via webhook and we will process it within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Shopify: We access your store data through Shopify's authenticated API. Shopify's privacy policy governs the platform itself.
  • Fly.io: Our hosting provider. Data remains within their US East infrastructure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be communicated via the Shopify Partner email on file.
