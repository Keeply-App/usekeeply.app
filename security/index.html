Security Incident Response Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

This document describes how Keeply responds to security incidents that may involve merchant or customer data.

1. Scope

This policy applies to any event that compromises or may compromise the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of Protected Customer Data stored or processed by Keeply — including customer names, email addresses, and order history.

2. Identify

We detect incidents through:

  • Automated alerts: Fly.io infrastructure alerts for unusual access patterns or service failures
  • Structured access logs: Every read/write of customer or order data is logged; anomalies are reviewable at any time
  • Merchant reports: Direct notification via email
  • Shopify notifications: Alerts from Shopify's Partner Dashboard

3. Contain

Upon detecting a confirmed or suspected incident:

  • Immediately revoke any compromised API keys, access tokens, or credentials
  • Take the affected service offline if necessary to prevent further exposure
  • Preserve logs for investigation (do not delete)
  • Assess the scope: which shops and customers are affected, what data was exposed

4. Notify

  • Affected merchants will be notified within 72 hours of a confirmed breach, via the email address on their Shopify account
  • Notification will include: what happened, what data was involved, what we have done, and what merchants should do
  • Shopify will be notified via the Partner Dashboard incident reporting channel
  • If required by applicable law (GDPR, etc.), relevant data protection authorities will be notified

5. Post-Mortem

Within 7 days of resolution:

  • Document the root cause
  • Document the timeline of detection, containment, and notification
  • Implement preventive measures to avoid recurrence
  • Update this policy if needed

6. Contact

To report a security issue: support@usekeeply.app

Response time: within 24 hours.
